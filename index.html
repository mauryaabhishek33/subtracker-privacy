Privacy Policy - SubTracker Smart Reminders

Last Updated: August 2025

Introduction

SubTracker Smart Reminders ("we", "our", "app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Android application.

Information We Don't Collect

We want to be clear about what we DON'T do:

  • We DON'T collect personal information like names, emails, or phone numbers
  • We DON'T track your location
  • We DON'T sell or share your data with third parties
  • We DON'T use your data for advertising
  • We DON'T upload your SMS messages to any server

Information Stored Locally

All your subscription data is stored locally on your device:

  • Subscription names and amounts
  • Payment due dates and frequencies
  • Notes and categories
  • Reminder preferences

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up.

Optional Permissions

SMS Access (Optional - Currently Disabled)

  • Status: Disabled in version 1.0 for Play Store compliance
  • Future Purpose: Auto-detect subscriptions from payment messages
  • Usage: When enabled, messages will be scanned locally on your device
  • Storage: We don't store your messages

Notifications (Optional)

  • Purpose: Send payment reminder notifications
  • Usage: Local notifications only, no push notifications
  • Requirement: You can use the app without notifications

Exact Alarm Permission

  • Purpose: Schedule precise reminder notifications
  • Usage: Ensures reminders work reliably even in battery saving mode
  • Requirement: Recommended for reliable reminders

Third-Party Services

Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to:

  • Identify and fix app crashes
  • Improve app stability
  • Monitor app performance

Data collected by Crashlytics:

  • Crash stack traces
  • Device model and OS version
  • App version
  • Installation UUID (not linked to you personally)

You can opt-out of crash reporting in the app settings.

Google Drive Backup (Optional)

  • Only activated if you choose to backup
  • Your data is encrypted before upload
  • Stored in your personal Google Drive
  • We cannot access your backed-up data

Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Local data encryption
  • No data transmission by default
  • Secure coding practices
  • Regular security updates

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data (visible in the app)
  • Delete all your data (Settings > Clear All Data)
  • Export your data (Settings > Export Data)
  • Opt-out of crash reporting
  • Use the app without optional permissions

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
